What is Green Justice?

Green Justice is a digital bridge between people and institutions. We turn citizen-reported environmental violations into structured complaints that reach the relevant council, committee, or department with all the details they need.

How it works

1. Citizens report

Witnesses select the type of violation, add a photo or video, share the location and provide any extra details.

2. Smart routing

The system links each report with the most relevant authority office, including contact details for quick follow-up.

3. Tracking & reminders

Authorities see a live list of complaints, sort them by urgency, and receive reminders if a report has not been addressed within a week.
